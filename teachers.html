About ARKA

FOR THE EDUCATOR OF THE 22nd Century.

The leaves and flowers of the ARKA plant find deep rooted references in the Indian holy scriptures. They also possess medicinal and healing powers. Rightly so the services offered by ARKA, the educational venture will steer forward the educator of the future to be a smart, well equipped, creative as well as inspiring personality. When a teacher is confronted with a misbehaving or a non- compliant student, the challenging behaviour presents a puzzle to be solved. Instructors skilled in resolving behavioural problems know that the disruptive behaviour can be resolved using certain methodologies. Dealing with extreme student behaviours can be a challenging experience for teachers, and diffusing small misdemeanours before they become big problems requires a skilful balance of concealing your emotions and using techniques to de-escalate the behaviour. ARKA, brings case studies together, portraying the behaviour of such students and help them to resolve the issues through different methodologies. She/He will be benefitted by the plethora of case studies, solutions and suggestions put forth by experienced individuals in the educational arena to handle real life situations that might arise in the classrooms. The services will be a guide to the educator both new and experienced to successfully navigate through her/his journey and prove to be a milestone in her/his success. These are the live experiences of our content developers who are associated in the field of education for more than 10 years. In such cases, ARKA empowers the teacher to combat such situations by giving them the awareness of unpredictable behaviour and also teaches them different techniques to handle challenging student behaviour and get better class room management.
